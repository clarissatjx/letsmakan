A gamified personality test that matches you with a Singaporean dish based on the 16 MBTI personality types. Inspired by the [Vegetal](https://github.com/sophie006liu/vegetal) personality test.

## About

Discover which iconic Singaporean dish represents your unique personality! Answer a series of questions and find out if you're a bold Chilli Crab, a comforting Laksa, a strategic Bak Kut Teh, or one of 13 other delicious Singaporean dishes.

## How It Works

1. Answer 10 questions about your preferences and behavior
2. The quiz scores your responses across the four MBTI dimensions:
   - **I/E**: Introversion vs Extroversion
   - **N/S**: Intuition vs Sensing
   - **F/T**: Feeling vs Thinking
   - **J/P**: Judging vs Perceiving
3. Get matched with your Singaporean dish personality
